## Overview

Perched on the serene slopes of Gangtok, **The Skyline Residency** represents the pinnacle of modern Himalayan living. Designed by CS Shingi & Associates, this project merges sustainable architecture with luxury amenities.

Every unit offers a **panoramic view of Kanchenjunga**, ensuring that nature is never more than a glance away.

### Key Features
* **Seismic Zone IV Compliant** Structure
* Heated Indoor Swimming Pool
* Smart Home Automation integration
* Private Terraces for every apartment

With Gangtok's real estate market growing at 12% annually, The Skyline Residency offers not just a home, but a secure asset for generations to come.
